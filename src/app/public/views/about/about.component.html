Our Story

Founded in 2010, HardwareHub began as a small local store with a simple mission: to provide high-quality hardware tools and materials at fair prices with exceptional customer service.

What started as a modest 1,000-square-foot shop has grown into a nationwide supplier of premium hardware products. Despite our growth, we've maintained our core values of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Today, we offer thousands of products from trusted brands, catering to both professional contractors and DIY enthusiasts. Our knowledgeable staff continues to provide the personalized service that has been our hallmark since day one.
